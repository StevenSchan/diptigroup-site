Awards & Honours

Prof. Dipti Srinivasan is a Fellow of IEEE and has received numerous international and institutional awards in recognition of her sustained contributions to artificial intelligence, power and energy systems, education, and professional service.

She is a frequent keynote and invited speaker at leading international conferences and forums.

Selected Awards and Honours

  • SG100 Women in Tech, Singapore (2025)
  • IEEE PES Wanda Reder Pioneer in Power Award (2022)
  • Outstanding Volunteer Award, IEEE Power & Energy Chapter, Singapore (2021)
  • Fellow, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), USA (2020)
  • Outstanding Volunteer Award, IEEE Singapore Section (2020)
  • Engineering Educator Award, National University of Singapore (2012, 2011)
  • IEEE PES Singapore Outstanding Engineer Award (2010)
  • Annual Teaching Excellence Award, National University of Singapore (2007, 2008, 2009, 2015 Honour Roll)
