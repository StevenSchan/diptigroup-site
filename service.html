Leadership & Service

Prof. Dipti Srinivasan has held extensive leadership roles at international, national, and institutional levels. She currently serves as a Board Member of the Energy Market Authority (EMA), Singapore, contributing to policy, regulatory, and strategic discussions on energy reliability and future grid development. She is also a member of the IEEE Power & Energy Society Governing Board.

Within IEEE, she has provided long-standing service across the Power & Energy Society and Computational Intelligence Society, including leadership roles in strategic planning, conference oversight, diversity and inclusion initiatives, education, awards, and fellows committees. Her contributions have helped shape research directions, professional development pathways, and global engagement across the AI and energy communities.
